A/B Testing

Testing Recommendations

4 steps

Initial Budget Allocation

Start with the highest-intent hooks. Allocate initial ad spend between Hot and Warm hooks to establish baseline performance before testing Cold.

60% Hot
40% Warm
60% -- Hot Hook (pain-aware audience)
40% -- Warm Hook (story-led audience)

Optimization Roadmap

  • 1
    Launch: Hot + Warm Hooks (Days 1-7)

    Run Hot Hook (60% budget) and Warm Hook (40% budget) simultaneously. Track cost per quiz start, cost per completion, and cost per lead. Let both run for a full 7 days before making changes.

  • 2
    Introduce Cold Hook (Day 8+)

    After 7 days, introduce the Cold Hook using the winning creative format from Step 1. If Hot Hook used image+badge, apply that same visual format to the Cold Hook. Allocate 20% of budget to Cold.

  • 3
    Build Lookalike Audiences (100+ Leads)

    Once you have 100+ quiz completers, create lookalike audiences from your completed leads. Layer lookalikes over interest targeting for best results. Prioritize hot-scored leads for the seed audience.

  • 4
    Scale Winners, Kill Losers (Ongoing)

    Review performance weekly. Scale hooks with cost-per-lead below your target. Pause any hook that fails to hit benchmarks after 14 days of data. Reallocate budget to top performers.
